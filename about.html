About

Alexandra Moya is a Hispanic artist that is based in Texas, currently pursuing a BFA in Painting and Ceramics at Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi. She works primarily in acrylic and often likes experimenting with different kinds of mediums and forms in some of her pieces such as the piece "Shroud" with contains a mix of 3D sculpture and insulation foam. Alex also takes interest in ceramic pottery and frequently experiments with her glazing techniques. Some of her works are composed of how and what she is feeling in the moment or what sparks her interests.
