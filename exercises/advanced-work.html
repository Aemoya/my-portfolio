3D Model: Astronaut

Artwork Metadata

Title: Cosmic Wanderer
Artist: Digital Dreamscapes Collective
Year: 2023
Medium: 3D Digital Sculpture, Interactive WebGL
Description:

This captivating 3D digital sculpture, "Cosmic Wanderer," invites viewers to contemplate the vastness of space and the human spirit of exploration. Rendered with meticulous detail, the astronaut symbolizes humanity's enduring quest for knowledge beyond our terrestrial confines. The interactive WebGL presentation allows for an immersive experience, enabling users to rotate, zoom, and pan around the model, discovering new perspectives and appreciating the intricate design from every angle. It serves as a testament to the boundless possibilities of digital art in the modern era.
